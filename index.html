    Powerful Management

    TicketForge isn't just a bot, it's a full CRM for Discord.

    • Data-Driven Decisions

      Visualize your support health with the Guild Statistics dashboard. Track ticket volume, response times, and peak hours to optimize your staffing.

    • Command Control

      Stop unauthorized actions. Use Command Management to strictly define which roles can use specific commands (like /close or /add) and in which channels.

    • Audit Logs

      Never lose track of a change. The Activity Log provides a detailed timeline of every configuration update, subscription change, and dashboard login.

    • Satisfaction Ratings

      Gather CSAT scores (1-5 Stars) and feedback after every ticket. Use this data to identify your top-performing support agents.


    Feature Highlights

    Custom Forms

    Collect essential data before a ticket opens. Create specific questionnaires and modals to ensure your staff has all the context they need immediately.

    Claiming System

    Prevent staff collisions. Moderators can "Claim" a ticket, locking it to their handling so two people don't answer the same user simultaneously.

    Private Threads

    Keep your channel list clean. TicketForge supports private Threads for ticket conversations, preventing channel bloat in large servers.

    Localization

    Speak your community's language. Customize the bot's responses and interface language to match your server's region.
